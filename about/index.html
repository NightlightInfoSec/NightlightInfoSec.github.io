ABOUT ME

I’m a SOC analyst trying to learn new things.

I had been thinking about creating a website for myself, but could never seem to come up with a good name.

Then one night after putting my kids to bed, I looked over at their nightlight and thought about all the times I have stayed up studying after putting them to bed.

I immediately checked to see if the name was registered, and that’s how NightlightInfoSec.com was born.
